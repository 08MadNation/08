Latest Track

Fresh-D – 242
Fresh-D’s latest track “242” is a vibrant tribute to his hometown, Brazzaville. Through rhythmic beats and sharp lyricism, he paints a vivid picture of the city’s cultural heartbeat. The track delves into the rich heritage, struggles, and triumphs of Brazzaville, offering listeners an authentic glimpse into the spirit of Congo’s capital. “242” not only celebrates the city’s impact on Fresh-D’s journey but also showcases the powerful connection between music and cultural identity. A must-listen for anyone looking to experience the soul of Brazzaville through sound.
