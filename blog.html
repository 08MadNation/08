Special Announcement

By Admin

We are excited to announce our new service packages at 08 Madnation, perfect for artists looking to take their tracks to the next level. With our specialized Mix and Mastering services, you can now enhance the sound quality of your music. As a special offer, your first song will be mixed and mastered for free! This is an incredible opportunity to experience professional sound engineering without any initial cost.

Our Basic Package includes mixing for up to 5 tracks and mastering for a single track, all for just ₱1,500. This package is ideal for those working on small projects or looking to improve the quality of their individual songs. Don't settle for less when you can get the best sound quality at an affordable rate.

If you're working on a larger project, consider our Standard Package. For ₱2,000, you'll get mixing for up to 10 tracks and mastering for a single track. Perfect for more complex projects, this package ensures that all your songs are given the same level of professional care and attention.

For the ultimate experience, try our Premium Package, which offers mixing for up to 20 tracks and mastering for a single track, all for ₱2,500. Plus, with every package, you will receive 2 free revisions for your first song, free vocal presets for leads and background vocals, and a free acapella mixed version. Let us help you make your music stand out!
